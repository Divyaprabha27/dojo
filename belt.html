Belt Progression

Road to Black Belt

"A black belt is a white belt who never gave up."

At Dragon Fist Dojo, we believe that rank is not just about the color of your belt, but the content of your character. Progression requires dedication, humility, and a constant drive to improve.

White Belt

0 - 6 Months

Focus: Fundamentals, Etiquette, Basic Stances.

The beginning of the journey. Students learn the dojo culture and the core movements that form the foundation of all martial arts.

Yellow Belt

6 - 12 Months

Focus: Balance, Coordination, Core Techniques.

Students begin to chain movements together and develop a deeper understanding of body mechanics.

Orange Belt

12 - 18 Months

Focus: Combinations, Sparring Drills, Speed.

Introduction to dynamic movement and partner drills. Improving reaction time is a key goal.

Green Belt

18 - 24 Months

Focus: Advanced Kicks, Grappling Basics, Power.

Students start to specialize and refine their techniques. Physical conditioning becomes more intense.

Brown Belt

2 - 3 Years

Focus: Strategy, Leadership, Live Sparring.

The final steps before mastery. Brown belts are expected to mentor junior students and demonstrate flawless technique.

Black Belt

3+ Years

Focus: Mastery, Teaching, Lifelong Learning.

Not the end, but a new beginning. A symbol of expertise and the responsibility to pass on knowledge.
